Akita Rescued from Abandoned Car Sparks Outpouring of Support

An Akita named Hachi was rescued from an abandoned car in Riverdale, thanks to the quick actions of local resident Sarah Jenkins. Noticing the dog motionless in the sweltering vehicle, Jenkins called animal control, who safely freed the dehydrated but resilient pup.

“It’s a miracle he survived,” said Officer James Miller, who led the rescue. Hachi was taken to Riverdale Animal Shelter, where veterinarians treated him and reported he was stable and recovering well.

Investigators are pursuing animal cruelty charges against the unidentified owner who left the car abandoned for over a week. Meanwhile, Hachi’s story has sparked an outpouring of support, with adoption offers and donations flooding the shelter.

“Everyone wants to give Hachi the home he deserves,” said shelter director Maria Lopez. For now, Hachi is recovering, surrounded by love and care, a symbol of resilience and community compassion.
